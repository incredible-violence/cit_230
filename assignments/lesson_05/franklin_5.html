Franklin Breaks Record

Franklin broke an all-time record this past weekend, in a category no one could have predicted. In a stunning turn of events, as of Saturday, Franklin has experienced more days of sun in a row than ever in recorded history. At no point in history has Franklin had so much sun. Frankly, it's getting tiresome, and may present a problem for the local farm industry which is reliant upon the rainfall to grow crops. One farmer is quoted as saying,"If we don't get a serious rain soon, we may need to look into irrigation or sprinkler systems, and no one around these here parts can afford such extravagances."
