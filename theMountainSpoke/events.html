Cycle the Continental Divide!

Join us for the Annual Event

Each year, participants come together to Cycle the Continental Divide! This is a great event because while it is challenging, it is not particularly dangerous. In fact, it is quite rare that someone will die on the trail. This makes it a great entry point for beginners who want to step to the next level. Its length makes it great for a test of endurance. In fact, this same track is used for the local Triathalon.

    Requirements:

  • Participants must bring own equipment or rent, no freebies here
  • Participants must sign insurance liability waiver
  • Participants must be at least 16 years of age or have a parent with them
  • Cost: $25

    • Proceeds will benefit the Local Wildlife Foundation
